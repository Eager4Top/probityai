Cookie Policy
Last updated: May 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how PROBITY AI uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website (this GitHub Pages site) during the OAuth social media verification flow.

💡 The PROBITY AI Telegram bot itself does not use cookies. Cookies are only relevant when your browser opens our website during the OAuth authorisation flow (e.g. when verifying your Facebook handle).

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored in your browser when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences or login state. We also use similar technologies like session storage for security purposes.

Cookies We Use

Cookie / StorageTypePurposeDuration

Third-Party Cookies (OAuth Platforms)

When you click "Log in with Facebook" or another platform's button, you are redirected to that platform's login page. Those platforms may set their own cookies in your browser according to their own cookie policies:

  • Facebook / Instagram: Facebook Cookie Policy
  • TikTok: TikTok Cookie Policy
  • X (Twitter): X Privacy Policy

PROBITY AI does not control or have access to cookies set by third-party platforms.

What We Do NOT Use Cookies For

  • Advertising or tracking across websites
  • Building user profiles for marketing purposes
  • Analytics beyond what is necessary for security
  • Any purpose unrelated to the OAuth verification flow

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling session cookies may prevent the OAuth social media verification flow from completing correctly, as the security state token is required.

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
