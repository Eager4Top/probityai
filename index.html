FAQ
Common Questions
Is PROBITY AI free to join? +
Yes. Registration is completely free for both Promoters and Merchants. Promoters earn USDT immediately after verifying their first social media handle. Merchants purchase ad bundles to run campaigns.
How does social media verification work? +
We use OAuth 2.0 — the same secure login standard used by "Sign in with Google" or "Login with Facebook". You log in on the platform's own official page. PROBITY AI never sees or stores your password. You can revoke access at any time from your social media settings.
When and how do I get paid? +
Rewards are credited to your PROBITY AI wallet after each post is verified (usually within 30 minutes). You can withdraw your USDT balance to any TON Network wallet at any time. The minimum withdrawal is 5 USDT with a 0.50 USDT network fee.
Will posting ads affect my social media account? +
PROBITY AI only posts content that has been approved and complies with each platform's community guidelines. You review what is posted before it goes live. You can disconnect any platform handle at any time with a single tap.
Which countries can use PROBITY AI? +
PROBITY AI is available globally. As long as you have a Telegram account and at least one social media handle, you can participate. Payments are made in USDT on the TON Network which is accessible worldwide.
How do Merchants get their ads distributed? +
Once a Merchant's ad is approved and active, PROBITY AI automatically notifies all eligible Promoters who have verified handles on the target platforms. Promoters can then post with a single tap. Merchants pay only per verified post — no wasted spend.
