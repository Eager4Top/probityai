Data Deletion Request
Last updated: May 2026  ·  Response within 30 days

PROBITY AI respects your right to have your personal data deleted. This page explains exactly what gets deleted, what is retained for legal reasons, and how to submit your request.

This page also serves as PROBITY AI's official Data Deletion Callback URL as required by Meta (Facebook & Instagram), TikTok, and X developer policies.

Request Account & Data Deletion

Open the PROBITY AI bot on Telegram and send a deletion request through our support system. Your request will be processed within 30 days.

Submit Deletion Request via Bot

Step-by-Step Deletion Process

  • 1

    Open the PROBITY AI Bot

    Tap the button above or search for @Probityads_Bot on Telegram and open a chat.

  • 2

    Go to Support

    In the bot menu, tap Support → New Ticket and select the "Account Deletion" ticket type.

  • 3

    Send Your Request

    In the ticket message write: "I request deletion of my account and all associated personal data."

  • 4

    Receive Confirmation

    You will receive a ticket confirmation number. Your data will be fully deleted within 30 days and you will be notified when complete.

What Gets Deleted

🗑 Permanently Deleted

  • Your Telegram ID, name, username, and phone number
  • All connected social media handles and usernames
  • All OAuth access tokens (encrypted) for all platforms
  • Your TON wallet address
  • Profile URL data and session tokens
  • Support ticket messages
  • Referral relationships
  • Registration session data

📋 Retained for Legal Compliance

  • Anonymized transaction records (required for financial compliance — 3 years)
  • Aggregated, non-identifiable ad performance statistics
  • Security incident logs (anonymized, 90 days)

⚠️ Deleting your account will forfeit any unwithdrawn USDT balance below the minimum withdrawal threshold (5 USDT). Please withdraw your balance before requesting deletion if you wish to retain your earned rewards.

Disconnecting a Single Platform

If you only want to disconnect one social media handle (rather than deleting your entire account):

  • Open the PROBITY AI bot → Social Handles → [Platform Name] → Update Handle
  • Or revoke PROBITY AI's access directly from your social media account settings (Facebook Settings → Apps & Websites, etc.)

Revoking access on the social media platform's side immediately invalidates PROBITY AI's stored OAuth token for that platform, which we then detect and clear automatically.

For Facebook / Instagram OAuth Disconnection

If you connected your Facebook or Instagram account to PROBITY AI and wish to revoke access:

  • Go to Facebook Settings → Security → Apps & Websites
  • Find PROBITY AI in the list
  • Click Remove

This immediately revokes our access token. Your data will be deleted from our servers within 30 days or immediately upon receiving a deletion request.

Processing Time

All deletion requests are processed within 30 days of receipt. You will receive a confirmation message via Telegram when deletion is complete. If you do not receive confirmation within 30 days, please resubmit your request.
