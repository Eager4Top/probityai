Overview & Scope

PROBITY AI ("we", "our", "us", "the Platform") operates a blockchain-powered social media advertising platform accessible through the Telegram messaging application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect the personal information of all users — Promoters and Merchants — who interact with our services.

By registering on PROBITY AI, connecting a social media handle, or using any feature of the platform, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Account Data (collected at registration):

  • Telegram User ID, name, and username
  • Phone number (provided during Telegram registration)
  • Email address (if provided)
  • Referral code (if applicable)
  • Account role: Merchant or Promoter

Social Media Data (collected when you connect a handle):

  • Social media handle / username for each connected platform
  • Encrypted OAuth 2.0 access tokens (AES-256-GCM encrypted at rest)
  • Public profile URL for the connected account
  • Platform-confirmed account identity (used for handle ownership verification)

Transaction & Activity Data:

  • Ad assignment records (which ads you posted, when, and on which platform)
  • USDT reward amounts credited per post
  • Withdrawal requests and TON wallet addresses
  • Daily post counts and verification status

Technical Data:

  • IP address (logged during OAuth callback to our server)
  • Browser/device type (during OAuth flow)
  • Server access logs (retained for security purposes)

Legal Basis for Processing

OAuth & Third-Party Access

🔒 PROBITY AI uses OAuth 2.0 for all social media connections. We never collect, store, or transmit your social media passwords. Authentication happens entirely on the platform's own official login pages.

When you tap a "Connect" button in PROBITY AI, your browser opens the official login page of that platform (e.g., facebook.com/login). After you approve access, that platform sends us an encrypted authorization code. We exchange this code for an access token which is immediately encrypted with AES-256-GCM before being stored. The access token is used exclusively to post approved ad content on your behalf.

Data Security

  • All OAuth access tokens are encrypted using AES-256-GCM before database storage
  • OAuth state parameters are HMAC-SHA256 signed to prevent CSRF attacks
  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • Server access controls and environment variable isolation for secrets
  • No passwords or sensitive credentials are ever logged
  • Credential messages sent by users during any flow are immediately deleted from Telegram

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for their own purposes.

We may disclose information only in these circumstances:

  • Legal obligation: If required by a court order, law enforcement request, or applicable regulation.
  • Security enforcement: To investigate fraud, abuse, or violations of our Terms of Service.
  • Platform APIs: Ad content is posted through official social media APIs — the platform receives the post content and your account access token as technically required.
  • TON blockchain: Withdrawal transactions are recorded on the public TON blockchain by design.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data. See our Data Deletion page for instructions.
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interest.
  • Withdraw Consent: Disconnect any social media handle or delete your account at any time.

To exercise any right, contact us via the PROBITY AI Telegram bot support system or visit our Data Deletion page.

Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active.
  • Transaction records are retained for 3 years for financial compliance purposes.
  • Upon account deletion, all personal data is removed within 30 days except where legal retention obligations apply.
  • OAuth tokens are deleted immediately upon disconnecting a social media handle.
  • Server logs are retained for 90 days for security purposes then deleted.

Children's Privacy

PROBITY AI is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed on servers located outside your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international transfers, consistent with GDPR and applicable data protection laws.
